Beyond the Hype – Building a Real Career in Data Science & AI

The KVG College of Engineering Open Source Community (KVGCE OSC) hosted an enlightening session titled “Beyond the Hype: Building a Real Career in Data Science & AI” on Saturday, October 11th, from 2:00 PM to 3:00 PM IST. The event aimed to go beyond the buzzwords of Artificial Intelligence and Data Science, helping students gain a realistic perspective on career growth in these emerging fields.


About the Speaker

The guest speaker, Ms. Himanshi Garg, Data Scientist at dentsu, shared her valuable experiences and industry insights. With years of expertise in data-driven solutions, she guided students on how to align their academic knowledge with practical applications.


Session Overview

The session commenced with a welcome address from the OSC coordinators, followed by a formal introduction of the speaker. Ms. Himanshi provided an in-depth understanding of how real-world AI projects function — from data preprocessing and model deployment to result interpretation and ethical AI practices.

She encouraged participants to strengthen their mathematical foundation, coding skills, and problem-solving approach, emphasizing that a true career in AI requires curiosity, consistency, and creativity. The Q&A segment at the end of the session allowed students to interact directly with Ms. Himanshi, who answered queries about industry tools, learning resources, and project ideas.



Participation and Engagement

The event saw active participation from students of various departments who showed keen interest in understanding AI’s real-world impact. The interactive nature of the session kept participants engaged throughout.



Conclusion

“Beyond the Hype” was a highly informative and motivational session, helping students view Data Science and AI as long-term, skill-driven career paths. The KVGCE Open Source Community extends its heartfelt gratitude to Ms. Himanshi Garg for sharing her expertise and time with the college community.


Acknowledgment

A special appreciation goes to the OSC coordinators and volunteers for their dedicated efforts in planning and executing the event seamlessly. Their teamwork contributed to the success of yet another inspiring learning experience.
