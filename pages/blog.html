KVGCEOSC Blog

Beyond the Hype – Building a Real Career in Data Science & AI”

The KVG College of Engineering Open Source Community (KVGCE OSC) hosted an enlightening session titled “Beyond the Hype: Building a Real Career in Data Science & AI” on Saturday, October 11th, from 2:00 PM to 3:00 PM IST. The event aimed to go beyond the buzzwords of Artificial Intelligence and Data Science, helping students gain a realistic perspective on career growth in these emerging fields.

Full Stack Web Development Workshop

The Department of Computer Science and Engineering, in association with the KVGCE Open Source Community (KVGCEOSC), organized a hands-on Workshop on Full Stack Web Development to bridge the gap between theoretical knowledge and practical implementation of web technologies. The primary objective of the event was to equip students with the skills required to design, develop, and deploy dynamic and responsive web applications using modern tools and frameworks. Full Stack Development is one of the most in-demand domains in the software industry today, and this workshop provided students an opportunity to explore both frontend and backend technologies under expert guidance.

STUDENT INDUCTION PROGRAM (SIP) – Batch 2025

The KVG College of Engineering Open Source Community (KVGCEOSC) successfully organized the Student Induction Program (SIP) for the 1st-year Batch 2025 to introduce students to open-source culture, technical skills, and personal development. The program aimed to guide newcomers as they begin their engineering journey.

Git in Action ” Workshop, Website & T-Shirt Launch

The KVG College of Engineering Open Source Community (KVGCEOSC) organized an engaging workshop titled “Git in Action” on May 15, 2025, at 2:00 PM in the Smart Classroom (406). The event aimed to provide students with a hands-on understanding of Git and GitHub — essential tools for modern software development and collaboration.

The Visionaries Behind Our Open Source Community

Our college's Open Source Community, KVGCEOSC, is led by a dedicated team, including President Nithish Vasanth, Vice President Durga Prasad N, and other key members who manage technical projects, events, finances, and marketing. Together, they create an inclusive space for students to learn, collaborate, and make impactful contributions. With their guidance, KVGCEOSC is set to drive innovation and growth in open-source development.

Tech Talk on Commercializing Academic Projects

On 25th November 2024, Our College hosted an insightful tech talk featuring Shreemukha Sullia, Founder and CEO of Buttistore.com. The session, titled "Commercializing Academic Projects," aimed to bridge the gap between academic research and entrepreneurial ventures, providing students with actionable strategies to transform their innovative ideas into successful businesses.

AI Workshop on Real-time AI Bridge and AI Fundamentals

On November 29, 2024, the KVGCE Open Source Community (KVGCEOSC) hosted an insightful workshop in collaboration with AIOTRIX Private Limited. The event, which took place at the KVGCE Auditorium, featured Kiran R. Shetty (Chief Operating Officer) and Karthik Suvarna (Product Manager) as the guest speakers. The workshop was a huge success, drawing enthusiastic participation from students and faculty alike.

Inauguration of Our College Open Source Community

Our college launched its Open Source Community, KVGCEOSC, on November 15, 2024, led by Principal Dr. Suresha V and CEO Dr. Ujwal U J. KVGCEOSC fosters collaboration and hands-on learning, inviting students to join impactful projects, workshops, and coding sessions. We look forward to building a collaborative digital future together with KVGCEOSC!
