About KVGCEOSC

KVGCEOSC (KVG College of Engineering Open Source Community) is a vibrant student-led technical community dedicated to fostering open-source culture and technological innovation. Founded with the vision of empowering students through practical learning and collaborative development, we serve as a platform where passion meets technology.

Our community brings together like-minded individuals who are passionate about technology, coding, and open-source contributions. We organize workshops, hackathons, coding competitions, and technical sessions to help students enhance their skills and stay updated with the latest technological trends.

Our Mission

To create an inclusive environment that encourages learning, innovation, and collaboration while contributing to the open-source ecosystem and developing technical skills among students.

Our Vision

To become a leading student community that bridges the gap between academic learning and industry requirements through practical exposure to open-source development and emerging technologies.

Our Core Values

Innovation

Encouraging creative solutions and new ideas

Collaboration

Working together to achieve common goals

Learning

Continuous growth and skill development

Inclusivity

Creating opportunities for everyone
